## 📄 Cover Letter 草稿

```
[Date]

Editor-in-Chief
IEEE Transactions on Pattern Analysis and Machine Intelligence

Dear Editor,

We are pleased to submit our manuscript entitled "Vision-Language-Action Models for Embodied AI: A Comprehensive Survey and Research Agenda" for consideration for publication in IEEE TPAMI.

**Significance**: This is the first systematic survey of Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models, covering 287 papers from 2023-2026. VLA models represent a paradigm shift in embodied AI, unifying visual perception, language understanding, and action generation.

**Key Contributions**:
1. First comprehensive VLA survey (287 papers analyzed)
2. VLA-Taxonomy: A unified classification framework (3 dimensions, 8 categories, 24 subcategories)
3. Quantitative technical analysis (performance, efficiency, cost)
4. Five core challenges identified with in-depth analysis
5. Future research agenda (5 open questions, 10 specific directions, roadmap)

**Why TPAMI**: This work aligns with TPAMI's scope in pattern analysis, machine intelligence, and comprehensive surveys. The systematic analysis and unified framework will serve as a foundational reference for the growing VLA research community.
```
