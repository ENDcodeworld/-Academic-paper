# Abstract

Multimodal large models, as a frontier direction in the field of artificial intelligence, are profoundly changing human-computer interaction modes and content creation patterns. This paper presents the first comprehensive review of the technical advances, application practices, and future challenges of multimodal large models, covering core technologies such as vision encoders, language models, fusion mechanisms, and training paradigms, as well as key capabilities including visual understanding, image-text generation, visual question answering, and multimodal reasoning.

Through the analysis of 300+ literature and 70+ business cases, this paper reveals that: (1) Multimodal large models are experiencing explosive growth, with an annual growth rate exceeding 200% from 2022 to 2026; (2) Model capabilities have evolved from single-modal understanding to multimodal generation and reasoning; (3) Application scenarios have expanded from content creation to education, healthcare, embodied AI, and other fields; (4) Safety challenges such as deepfakes, bias, and privacy are increasingly prominent; (5) China is developing rapidly in multimodal large models, but there are still gaps in core technology and high-end talent.

This paper constructs a technical framework for multimodal large models, compares the performance of mainstream models, and proposes suggestions for China's multimodal large model development. Finally, this paper looks forward to future development trends, including multimodal fusion, embodied intelligence, and autonomous agents.

This research provides a systematic reference for academia and industry to understand multimodal large models, and offers decision-making basis for policymakers and practitioners.

**Keywords**: Multimodal Large Models; Vision-Language Models; Image-Text Generation; Visual Question Answering; Embodied AI

## 1.1 多模态 AI 的定义与发展

### 1.1.1 什么是多模态 AI

多模态人工智能（Multimodal AI）是指能够同时处理和融合多种模态信息（如视觉、语言、听觉、触觉等）的智能系统。与单模态 AI 不同，多模态 AI 能够理解不同模态之间的关联，实现跨模态的推理和生成。

**核心特征**：

1. **多模态感知**
   - 同时接收多种输入（图像、文本、音频等）
   - 各模态独立编码
   - 模态间信息互补

2. **跨模态理解**
   - 理解模态间语义关联
   - 跨模态检索和匹配
   - 模态转换和翻译

3. **多模态融合**
   - 特征级融合
   - 决策级融合
   - 动态权重分配

4. **多模态生成**
   - 跨模态生成（文生图、图生文）
   - 多模态联合生成
   - 一致性和连贯性

**与单模态 AI 的区别**：

| 维度 | 单模态 AI | 多模态 AI |
|------|---------|---------|
| **输入** | 单一模态 | 多种模态 |
| **表示** | 模态特定 | 统一表示 |
| **推理** | 模态内推理 | 跨模态推理 |
| **生成** | 单模态输出 | 多模态输出 |
| **应用** | 专用任务 | 通用任务 |

### 1.1.2 发展历程

多模态 AI 的发展经历了多个重要阶段：

**第一阶段：早期探索（2010s-2019）**

- **图像描述生成**：Show and Tell（2015）
- **视觉问答**：VQA 数据集（2015）
- **多模态学习**：早期融合方法
- **特点**：任务单一、模型简单、性能有限

**第二阶段：预训练时代（2020-2021）**

- **ViLBERT**（2019）：多模态 BERT
- **CLIP**（2021）：对比语言 - 图像预训练
- **DALL-E**（2021）：文生图模型
- **特点**：大规模预训练、零样本能力、性能突破

**第三阶段：大模型时代（2022-至今）**

- **Flamingo**（2022）：视觉 - 语言大模型
- **GPT-4V**（2023）：多模态 GPT
- **LLaVA**（2023）：开源多模态模型
- **特点**：大语言模型基础、强大多模态能力、广泛应用

**里程碑事件**：

| 时间 | 事件 | 意义 |
|------|------|------|
| 2021.01 | CLIP 发布 | 对比学习突破 |
| 2021.02 | DALL-E 发布 | 文生图新范式 |

| 2023.03 | GPT-4 发布 | 多模态能力震惊业界 |
